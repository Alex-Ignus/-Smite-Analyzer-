General Overview

The goal of this visualization is to introduce players to the idea of the 16 Pantheon's and how often you as a player may play against a certain Pantheon.
By Hovering over any of the bars on the graph you'll gain further insights into each Pantheon the most played God the number of available gods that a player can choose from for that given Pantheon and how many matches most played goddess played
